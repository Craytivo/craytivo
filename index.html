Quick FAQ

How much do small business website packages cost?

Small business website packages start at $450. Final quote depends on scope and timeline. Minimum engagement for one-off support starts at $150.

How long does it take?

Some sites can be completed in as little as 4 days. Larger projects usually launch in a few weeks based on scope and feedback speed.

What happens after I submit?

You get a reply within 1 business day with next steps and options to schedule your call.

What affects total website cost?

Cost is shaped by page count, content readiness, custom features, integrations, and how quickly approvals are provided.

Do you require a deposit before starting?

Yes. Most projects start with a deposit to reserve your timeline and kickoff planning. Remaining payments are split across clear delivery milestones.

How many revisions are included?

Most packages include structured revision rounds. Exact revision scope is confirmed in your quote so expectations stay clear before build starts.

How do payment milestones work?

Payments are milestone-based, usually across kickoff, build progress, and final launch handoff.

Do I own the website content and files after launch?

Yes. After final payment, you retain ownership of your approved site content and project deliverables.

What is included in a typical build?

Most projects include mobile-first design, core SEO setup, lead forms, analytics setup, and a clear conversion-focused page structure.

Do you build on WordPress, static, or Shopify?

Yes. Platform recommendation depends on your goals, editing needs, and budget. I will suggest the most practical option during planning.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes. Ongoing support is available for updates, fixes, performance improvements, and post-launch optimisation.

Do you only work in Edmonton?

Edmonton and Alberta are my core focus, and I also support remote clients when there is a clear scope and communication process.
